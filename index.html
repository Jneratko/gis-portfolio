Flood Prediction Analysis - Okitibbeha County, Mississippi

The model utilized in the creation of this map contained four variables that each contributed to an output value, ranging from 0 to 3, representing the likelihood of flash flooding at each cell location. The four variables used were: land cover type, slope, distance to roads, and distance to rivers. The land cover type and distance to roads both contributed 30% to the overall likelihood while slope and distance to rivers both contributed 20%. The regions with the highest risk of flooding are roadways, especially where rivers and roads intersect.
